PLAN
1. Initiate levothyroxine 75 mcg daily (PO, empty stomach, 30–60 min before eating).

4. Ferrous sulfate 325 mg daily; separate from levothyroxine by 4 hours.
5. Refer to GYN for menorrhagia evaluation once thyroid treated.
