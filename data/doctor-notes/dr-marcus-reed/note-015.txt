3. Thrombocytopenia — mild; EBV-related; monitor
4. Amoxicillin allergy note: EBV + amoxicillin typically causes a diffuse maculopapular

PLAN
1. Supportive care — NO antibiotics (viral illness). Rest; adequate hydration; analgesics.
2. Acetaminophen 1 g Q6H for fever and pain. Ibuprofen 600 mg TID with food for inflammation.
3. Throat pain: Warm salt water gargles; cold/soft foods; popsicles; viscous lidocaine

7. Repeat LFTs and CBC in 3–4 weeks.
8. Return immediately to ER for: severe difficulty breathing, inability to swallow saliva,
